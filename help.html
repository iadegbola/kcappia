HOME



• Now that you have succesfully joined Kcappia, the first thing is to share your applications or games with your friends.

Then you will tell the os type so that the user can download applications of their own device, then you put your shared application name and you share it with your friends so that the world can enjoy the application or game with you.
When you are done you will then be able to see your friends shared applications also by following them. You can also share screenshots of app and games moments you enjoyed, share trillers of upcoming apps or games or their new updates, and you can also talk about apps or games you just finish testing. It is that simple.
