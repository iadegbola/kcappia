About Kcappia

Kcappia is an appsharing service that brings everything that you ever imagined to you. It can be used to share, talkabout, and share images and trillers of apps. Let us just say it makes app sharing more easy and fun.

It started in July 2017, and was created by ANPILOT Technology Creators, a company aimed at giving solutions to simple everyday problems. It co-founders are:

Onimago Basheer and Adegbola Ibukun

16 and 15 years old web developers who are trying to solve problems associated with technology. Kcappia was built on the foundation of giving all the ability to be able to do something they have always imagined doing, which is app and game sharing without restrictions and boundaries. Kcappia is built by people who are aimed at improving it.
